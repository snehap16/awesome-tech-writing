About This Framework

This comprehensive audit checklist standardizes documentation quality across engineering and content teams. It ensures every document is accurate, accessible, and ready for production.

Manual Review: Use this checklist as a step-by-step guide during peer reviews or self-edits. It helps you verify technical details, such as API placeholders, and confirms that content meets global accessibility standards.

AI Integration: Use the AI Review Panel below to automatically audit any document. Paste your content (or upload a file), optionally add a custom prompt, and Claude will fill in the checklist for you.
