About This Framework

This comprehensive audit system was designed to standardize documentation quality across engineering and content teams. It bridges the gap between technical accuracy and linguistic precision, ensuring every document is production-ready.

Manual Review: Use this as a step-by-step guide during peer reviews or self-edits to ensure no detail—from API placeholders to global accessibility—is overlooked.

AI Integration: To automate your workflow, export the JSON configuration of this checklist and feed it into your LLM (like Gemini or GPT-4) as a system prompt. By providing this structured rubric, you can instruct AI agents to pre-audit your Markdown files for style, structure, and AI-readiness before the final human review.
